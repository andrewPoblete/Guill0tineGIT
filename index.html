Guillotine

    The revolutionary card game where you win by getting a head

    Objective

    Collect the most points by beheading important nobles

    Overview

    Play is divided into 3 days. On each day, 10 nobles are lined up to await their turns at the guillotine. Players take turns playing action cards from their hands (possibly rearranging the order of the line) and then collecting nobles from the front of the line.

    Play

    1. Play an action card (optional)
    2. Collect the first noble in line by clicking the Executioner
    3. A new action card will be provided (whether or not you played one).
